Experience Ancient Egypt in One Day

Embark on an unforgettable journey through 3,000 years of history. Our private Luxor day trip from Hurghada takes you to Egypt's most iconic ancient sites, including the magnificent Karnak Temple, the striking Luxor Temple, and the mysterious Valley of the Kings where pharaohs were buried.

Detailed Itinerary

6:00 AM - Hotel Pickup

Our driver collects you from your hotel in Hurghada. Settle in for a scenic 4-hour drive to Luxor, enjoying views of the Egyptian landscape.

10:00 AM - Karnak Temple Complex

Arrive at the magnificent Karnak Temple, the largest temple complex in Egypt. Explore the Great Hypostyle Hall with 134 columns, the Sacred Lake, and numerous pylons while your guide explains the history and mythology.

12:30 PM - Lunch Break

Enjoy a delicious lunch at a local restaurant with views of the Nile. Try authentic Egyptian cuisine while relaxing.

2:00 PM - Luxor Temple

Visit the elegant Luxor Temple on the banks of the Nile. Marvel at the colossal statues of Ramses II and explore the hieroglyphic inscriptions.

3:30 PM - Valley of the Kings

Cross to the west bank and explore the Valley of the Kings. Visit 2-3 royal tombs (including the famous KV62 - Tutankhamun's tomb) and learn about ancient burial practices.

5:30 PM - Return Journey

Begin your scenic drive back to Hurghada, arriving around 9:30 PM. Reflect on an unforgettable day of ancient wonders.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the tour suitable for children?

Yes! Children over 6 years old can enjoy this tour. The sites are easily accessible and our guide can adjust the pace for younger travelers.

What's the best time to visit Luxor?

October to March offers the most comfortable temperatures. Avoid summer months (May-September) as temperatures exceed 40°C (104°F).

Can we visit more tombs in the Valley of the Kings?

You can visit up to 3 tombs. Additional tombs require separate tickets. Speak with our guide about your preferences.

Is photography allowed?

Photography is allowed at temples but may be restricted in some tombs (additional fees apply for camera use inside tombs).

What if I have mobility issues?

The temples have uneven surfaces but are navigable with assistance. Please inform us in advance so we can arrange support.

Can I customize the tour?

Absolutely! Let us know your interests and we can adjust the itinerary accordingly.
