Experience the Thrill of Desert Adventure

Detailed Itinerary

3:00 PM - Hotel Pickup

We collect you from your hotel and transport you to the desert starting point.

3:30 PM - Quad Bike Training & Departure

Brief safety training on quad bikes, then an exciting ride through golden dunes for 1.5 hours.

5:00 PM - Bedouin Camp & Camel Ride

Arrive at an authentic Bedouin camp. Meet local Bedouins and enjoy a traditional camel ride through the desert.

6:00 PM - Sunset & Relaxation

Experience the magical desert sunset from the camp with refreshments and hospitality.

7:00 PM - Traditional Bedouin Dinner

Enjoy an authentic feast including grilled meat, fresh bread, salads, and traditional drinks.

8:30 PM - Return to Hotel

Scenic drive back to your hotel, arriving around 9:30 PM.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need experience to ride a quad bike?

No experience necessary! We provide a brief safety training before the ride. Our guides ensure everyone's safety.

Is the tour suitable for children?

Children 12+ can participate. Younger children can enjoy the camel ride and camp experience without quad biking.

What if I'm not physically fit?

The tour is moderate intensity. If you have concerns, please let us know and we can adjust the experience.

Are helmets and safety gear provided?

Yes, safety helmets and protective gear are provided with all quad bikes.

What's the food like?

Authentic Bedouin cuisine including grilled meat, fresh bread, salads, and traditional sides.
