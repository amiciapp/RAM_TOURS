About RAM TOURS

Your Local Guide to Egypt's Wonders

Meet Your Local Guide

Welcome to RAM TOURS! I'm a passionate local guide with 10+ years of experience sharing the magic of Egypt with travelers worldwide. Based in Hurghada on Sheraton Street, I specialize in creating personalized, unforgettable experiences that go beyond typical tourist itineraries.

Whether you seek the mystique of ancient Luxor, the thrill of desert safaris, Red Sea snorkeling, or Cairo's pyramids, I guide you with insider knowledge and authentic stories.

My mission is simple: help you discover the real Egypt, connect with its rich history, and create lifetime memories while ensuring your safety and satisfaction are my top priorities.
