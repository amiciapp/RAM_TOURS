Stand Before the Pyramids of Giza

Experience one of the world's most magnificent archaeological wonders. Our Cairo day trip from Hurghada takes you to the iconic Pyramids of Giza, where you'll stand in awe of the Great Pyramid of Khufu, explore the mysterious Sphinx, and discover the treasures of the Egyptian Museum.

This is the ultimate bucket-list experience in Egypt, combining ancient mysteries with world-class museums in one unforgettable day.

Detailed Itinerary

5:00 AM - Early Pickup

We collect you early from your hotel to maximize daylight hours. Enjoy a comfortable 5-hour drive to Cairo with rest stops along the way.

10:00 AM - Giza Plateau

Arrive at the Giza Plateau and witness the awe-inspiring Pyramids of Khufu, Khafre, and Menkaure. Take photos and explore at your own pace with your guide.

11:30 AM - The Great Sphinx

Visit the iconic Sphinx with its mysterious expression. Learn theories about its construction and purpose while enjoying the surrounding panorama.

1:00 PM - Lunch Break

Enjoy lunch at a quality restaurant with pyramid views. Try authentic Egyptian and international cuisine.

2:30 PM - Egyptian Museum

Explore the world's greatest collection of ancient Egyptian artifacts. See the treasures of Tutankhamun, royal mummies, and countless ancient wonders.

5:30 PM - Return Journey

Begin your scenic drive back to Hurghada, arriving around 10:30 PM with unforgettable memories.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can we climb the pyramids?

Climbing the pyramids is not permitted for safety and preservation reasons. However, you can get excellent photos and explore the surrounding area.

How long is the drive from Hurghada to Cairo?

The drive takes approximately 5-6 hours each way depending on traffic and road conditions.

Is the Egyptian Museum air-conditioned?

Yes, the museum is air-conditioned and provides a comfortable environment for exploring the artifacts.

Can I visit other Cairo sites?

We can customize the itinerary. Other options include Islamic Cairo, Coptic Cairo, or the Citadel. Let us know your interests!

Is this tour suitable for seniors?

Yes, with some mobility considerations. Please inform us if you need assistance, and we'll make appropriate arrangements.

What's the best time to visit?

October to March offers comfortable temperatures. Avoid summer (May-September) when temperatures exceed 35°C (95°F).
