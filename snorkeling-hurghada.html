Detailed Itinerary

8:00 AM - Hotel Pickup

We collect you from your hotel and transport you to the marina.

8:30 AM - Boat Departure

Board our comfortable boat and sail to the first snorkeling site at Orange Bay.

9:30 AM - First Snorkeling Session

Enjoy 1.5 hours of snorkeling in crystal clear waters with abundant marine life.

11:00 AM - Second Location

Sail to another pristine reef location for more snorkeling and exploration.

12:30 PM - Beach Lunch

Relax on a beautiful beach and enjoy grilled fish, chicken, fresh salads and drinks.

2:00 PM - Final Snorkeling

One last snorkeling session at another spectacular reef site.

4:00 PM - Return to Hotel

Boat returns to marina, then transfer back to your hotel by 5:00 PM.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to know how to swim?

Basic swimming ability is recommended. We provide life jackets and support for all guests.

Is snorkeling equipment provided?

Yes, snorkels, masks, and fins are provided. You can also bring your own.

Can children participate?

Yes! Children 4+ can snorkel with supervision. Younger children can enjoy the beach.

What marine life will I see?

You'll see tropical fish, parrotfish, angelfish, rays, and colorful corals. Dolphins sometimes appear!

Is it safe?

Yes, our guides are certified and prioritize safety. Life jackets are available.
